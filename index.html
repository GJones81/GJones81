Grocery Gopher - Express Application

This is an express app for saving, editing, and using a grocery list while shopping. It is styled with a mobile-first mindset. This app makes use of two tables to manage the grocery list. The active_list table is for the current list only, and is designed so there can't be duplicate entries. The inventory_list is designed to allow for duplicate entries. The presence of duplicate entires is used to create a list of all items purchased twice or more in the last 30 days.

Mushroom Hunter

To begin play simply click on the button labeled ‘click here to get your flashlight and enter the mine’. Carefully search for the mushroom, and click on it. Clicking on the mushroom gives you one point and ends the level. Click on the button labeled ‘click here to continue’ to go to the next level. Starting at the second level a rat is randomly placed in the game area. Each level after the second level adds one more rat. There’s also a pause designed into the game when you scroll over the rats. As you progress in levels the pause decreases until it’s roughly 1/5 of a second. If you let your ‘flashlight’ linger too long on the rats they’ll attack you and you'll lose a health point.
